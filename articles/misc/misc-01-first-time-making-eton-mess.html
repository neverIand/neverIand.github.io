My British friend recommended me this dessert since berries are in-season right now. Also it's very easy to make, which is definitely a good news to someone like me who barely knows how to cook (or bake). This is the receipe I used: Strawberry Eton Mess (Easy, No-Bake Dessert) 

400g strawberries

4 meringue cookies (3 or even 2 should be enough)

3-4 Tbsp sugar

300ml whipping cream

The meringue cookies I got from Morrisons are huge (see the photo below) so I smacked more cookies than needed. I also forgot to the the lemon juice but luckily (or unluckily) the strawberry I got was not that sweet, so I guess it balanced out.

To me, the hardest part is actually whipping the cream. I have never whipped cream before myself, only watched others doing it. First time was a failure, the cream barely changed its form after several minutes of whipping and stiring. I then took a closer look at the tutorial and tried to shake the mixer a little bit when whipping, and eventually it worked.

It was really good, and since I still have some whipping cream left, I'll probably make it again with some other berries as well.
